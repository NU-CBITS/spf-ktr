How Can I Safely Have a Tan?

Listen

Some people prefer “a bit of color” to the skin. There is a safe alternative to tanning booths or lying out on the beach. Sunless, self-tanning creams and spray tans can darken the skin without increasing your risk of skin cancer.

You can find these creams at your local grocery store or pharmacy. A professional at a salon can also apply a spray tan. Be aware that even though you look like you have a tan, you need to practice sun protection just as much as you always do. The tan color is not protective against sun exposure.

IMPORTANT!

Do not use tanning booths because the artificial light is even more damaging than the sun.
